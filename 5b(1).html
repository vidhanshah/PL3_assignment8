SRH vs RCB IPL Dream11 Prediction, Match Preview, Pitch Report and Playing XI

Sunrisers Hyderabad and Royal Challengers are all set to meet in the third match of the Dream11 IPL 2020. While SRH have a string of outstanding bowlers in their ranks, RCB have a batting lineup that can dismantle legacies. This encounter promises to be an intriguing one. Both SRH and RCB have been active in the trade window and have done some smart business. The Hyderabad-based franchise have added Priyam Garg, Mitchell Marsh, Virat Singh and Fabian Allen among others. RCB have added Aaron Finch, Chris Morris, Kane Richardson, Dale Steyn, and Isuru Udana among others.
